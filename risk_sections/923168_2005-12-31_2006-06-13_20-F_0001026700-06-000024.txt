Risk Factors The nature of the business and industry that the Company operates is very seasonal. This seasonality combined with customer sales concentration and the reliance of consumers using their discretionary disposable income on fashion related products within current economic conditions compounded by the increase in cost of the gold componet of our product presents a potential risk that continued growth may be impacted. The majority of the products that the Company sells are imported from the Peoples Republic of China ("PRC") and Hong Kong and as such reliance on non restrictive trading agreements between the United States of America and these countries is critical.
