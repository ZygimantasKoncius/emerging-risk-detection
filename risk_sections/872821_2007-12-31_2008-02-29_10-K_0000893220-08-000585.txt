These protections could discourage potential acquisition proposals, or delay or prevent a change in control of our company. These provisions Page 3 require us to have a classified Board of Directors, require shareholders to inform us if they nominate directors, and give us the ability to issue up to 1 million shares of preferred stock and 150 million shares of common stock.
